The Best Movies According to Alpy

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Shawshank Redemption

This movie is the movie that tells how injustice can be reversed with patience, passion for hope and hard work.

Batman: The Dark Knight Rises

In this movie, Joker character and his quotes shine as the explanation of many questions that we encounter in our lives.

LOTR Trilogy

This trilogy is one of the best representation of the outcomes of absolute and limitless power.
